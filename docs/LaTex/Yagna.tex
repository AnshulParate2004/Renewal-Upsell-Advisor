\section{Platform Overview}
Yagna iQ is a comprehensive \textbf{Channel Ecosystem and Automation Platform} designed to eliminate manual processes and recurring revenue leakage in the distribution channel. It digitally connects Vendors, Distributors, Resellers, and End Customers on a secure cloud environment, facilitating seamless channel transactions for Leads, Quotes, Orders, Invoices, and Payments.

\subsection{Core Value Proposition}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{One-Stop SaaS Solution:} Replaces fragmented tools with a unified platform for Renewal Automation, Channel CPQ, and Lead Management.
    \item \textbf{Zero-Touch Automation:} Transforms "reactive" renewal quoting into a "proactive," automated process.
    \item \textbf{Long-Tail Monetization:} specifically targets low-value, high-volume transactions that are often neglected due to manual processing costs.
    \item \textbf{Security Compliance:} ISO 27001 certified, CyberGRX assessed, and Cisco CASPAR validated.
\end{itemize}

\section{Module 1: Renewal Cloud (Recurring Revenue Automation)}
\textit{Persona Targets: Vendor, Distributor, Reseller}

\subsection{Problem Statement}
The "1000 Portal Problem": Channel partners struggle with disparate vendor portals and manual data entry (Excel/Email), leading to reactive quoting, delays, errors, and lost revenue. A manual renewal quote process can take hours of labor and weeks to deliver.

\subsection{Key Features}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Proactive Quotes:} Automates the creation and delivery of renewal quotes 90/60/30 days in advance.
    \item \textbf{Multi-Vendor Aggregation:} Consolidates renewal data from multiple vendors into a single dashboard.
    \item \textbf{Zero-Touch Workflow:} Automates quote generation, margin application, currency conversion, and tax calculation.
    \item \textbf{Flexible Journeys:} Customizable workflows for different partner types and renewal scenarios (e.g., Low-touch vs. High-touch).
    \item \textbf{End-Customer Automation:} Allows resellers to send white-labeled, automated renewal notifications directly to end customers with embedded payment links.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Quantifiable Benefits (ROI)}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Efficiency:} Saves at least \textbf{125 hours} for every 100 contracts/subscriptions processed.
    \item \textbf{Revenue Growth:} Increases On-Time Renewal Rate by \textbf{20-25\%}.
    \item \textbf{Conversion:} Boosts conversion rates for low-dollar contracts by \textbf{10-15\%}.
    \item \textbf{Productivity:} Shifts workforce focus from "laborious follow-ups" to "efficient deal closure."
\end{itemize}

\section{Module 2: XSUS Cloud (Cross-Sell \& Upsell)}
\textit{Persona Targets: Vendor, Distributor, Reseller}

\subsection{Problem Statement}
Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) is rising (5-25x more expensive than retention). Businesses struggle to monetize their existing Install Base (IB) due to a lack of data analytics and visibility into white-space opportunities.

\subsection{Key Features}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Smart Lead Generation:} Uses Data Mining and AI/ML to sift through Install Base data.
    \item \textbf{Tech Refresh Identification:} Automatically identifies End-of-Life (EOL) products and suggests modern replacements.
    \item \textbf{White-Space Analysis:} Identifies missing product attach opportunities (e.g., "Customer has Router, needs Security License").
    \item \textbf{Actionable Quotes:} Generates leads that come with a pre-configured quote, not just contact info.
    \item \textbf{Perpetual to Subscription:} Identifies opportunities to migrate legacy perpetual licenses to recurring subscription models.
\end{itemize}

\section{Module 3: Channel CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote)}
\textit{Persona Targets: Vendor, Distributor, Reseller}

\subsection{Problem Statement}
Vendor CPQ tools are often internal-only (CRM-based) and do not extend to channel partners. Partners are forced to use Excel or Email for complex configurations, leading to errors and lack of pipeline visibility.

\subsection{Key Features}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Multi-Vendor CPQ:} Enables distributors/resellers to build quotes containing products from multiple vendors in a single cart.
    \item \textbf{Guided Selling:} Questionnaire-based configuration to ensure 100\% accurate quotes.
    \item \textbf{Advanced Pricing Engine:} Supports Volume-based, Attribute-based, Contracted, and Goal-Seek pricing.
    \item \textbf{Discount Management:} Handles complex discount stacking and approval authority hierarchies.
    \item \textbf{Subscription Support:} Native handling of subscription and bundle pricing logic.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Benefits}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Speed:} Faster time-to-market for new product bundles.
    \item \textbf{Accuracy:} Eliminates manual configuration errors and rework.
    \item \textbf{Compliance:} Enforces regulatory and commercial rules automatically.
\end{itemize}

\section{Module 4: Predictive WiFi Site Survey}
\textit{Target: Networking Vendors & Partners}

\subsection{Overview}
A cloud-based tool that eliminates the need for expensive, onsite RF engineering visits during the pre-sales phase.

\subsection{Capabilities}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Auto-Detect Walls (Beta):} Automatically identifies walls from uploaded floor plans.
    \item \textbf{environment Simulation:} Simulates RF propagation based on building materials (Concrete, Glass, Drywall).
    \item \textbf{Automatic AP Placement:} AI algorithms place Access Points (APs) for optimal coverage (RSSI, SNR).
    \item \textbf{Multi-Vendor Support:} Supports Cisco, Aruba, Ruckus, Extreme, Huawei, Ubiquiti, Aerohive.
    \item \textbf{BOM Generation:} Automatically creates a Bill of Materials (BOM) based on the survey results, usable in the CPQ module.
    \item \textbf{Google Maps Integration:} Locate buildings and define target coverage dimensions.
\end{itemize}
